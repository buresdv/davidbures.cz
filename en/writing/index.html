The Feng Dynasty (CZ)

Historical fantasy

The Hwazha Empire is in turmoil. If the new emperor, young Hwoniao Isarkiv Feŋ, wants to keep his title and head, he needs to hold his fragile empire together. His closest ally, the queen of a nation that has spent the last thousand years isolated underground, aids him in this almost pointless struggle.
But not everyone shares the new emperor’s vision of unity and stability; the chancellor of the Lia Commandery, the powerful Caoyon, seems to have a vision of his own. And the young emperor does not fit into it. And he will stop at nothing to see his goals reached.
